The Life of Enzo Ferrari

“I build engines and attach wheels to them.” -E. Ferrari

( Ferrari Quotes )

Enzo Ferrari was an italian motor racer and entrepreneur, who was also the founder of the Ferrari automobile enterprise. He is my idol because no matter what he did, he always made sure to come out at the top. Having the passion for racing, he founded the Ferrari racing team and sooner or later, Ferrari itself.

Racing Career

Enzo first started getting into racing cars when he was just 10 years old,when his dad took him to watch a motor car race in Bologna. He moved to Milan, Italy and started as a test driver for Costruzioni Meccaniche Nazionali, competing against the company's team. The following year, he left them for Alfa Romeo. In 1929, he pulled together his own team of drivers and engineers for his Scuderia Ferrari racing team. Ferrari competed in his final race in August 1931, since he had married Laura Dominica Garello and has become a father with the birth of his beloved son Dino in January 1932.

Ferrari starts his company of race cars.

After leaving Alfa Romeo, he decided that he wanted to design his own race cars. In March 1947 he took the first official Ferrari, the 125 S, out for a test-drive. The car scored its first win that year, at the Rome Grand Prix. It even went on to gain victories at the Mille Miglia in 1948, the 24 Hours of Le Mans in 1949 and the British Grand Prix in 1951.

Ferrari's last days

Enzo Ferrari died on August 14, 1988, in Maranello. No cause of death was given, but he was known for having a kidney disease.
